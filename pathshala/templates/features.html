One to One Mentorship

Provides students with an opportunity to study with the best mentors as well as gain insight on their performance. A study shows that 71.58% of students opt for home tutors which proves the likeliness of students towards private mentors.

  • Personalized attention from a faculty of Tier 1 Institute of India.
  • Year long course with Olympiads every month to track students performance on an all India level.
  • AI based feedback to improve on weaker concepts.
  • 24*7 doubt clearing facility.

Accessibilty and Flexibility

This platform makes tuition flexible and accessible where students can study and have a tutor staying at their home and as per their convenience

  • This is a personalised approach for students where a qualified teacher would be there for the student to constantly push and give daily targets which will act as a motivation for them which students usually lack
  • For both students and teachers, it is very easy to register themselves on our platform
  • During pandemic like these, parents would want their child to study at home as much as possible, this is the perfect platform for that where they can choose a teacher as per his/her qualification
